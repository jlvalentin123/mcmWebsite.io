BACCALAUREATE

1. E.T. YUCHENGCO INSTITUTIONAL SCHOLARSHIP

Sponsor: Malayan Colleges Mindanao (A Mapúa School), Inc.

Description: The scholarship is given to incoming baccalaureate students who have graduated with a GWA of 90% or above and have passed the interview. The scholarship is subject to the availability of slots.

Benefits:

• Free full tuition, L/F/D, and miscellaneous fees

• Learning materials worth P2,000 per term

Retention Policy:

• 12 academic units of course load for trimester /

• 18 academic units of course load for the semester /

• 15 academic units of course load for night curriculum /

Must obtain a credit term average of 2.00 or better at the end of the term or semester.

Qualifications: Income of (both) parent/s must not exceed P300,000

Requirements: Copy of BIR 1701 form or any proof of annual income

Note: Please bring the original copy of your documents for cross-checking.

2. E.T. YUCHENGCO INSTITUTIONAL SCHOLARSHIP SCREENING PROCEDURE

Applicants must be an official applicant of MCM. To become an official applicant, they must have completed the application procedure from Steps 1 to 3:

• Step 1: Accomplish the online applicant registration. If not yet, apply thru this link: https://bit.ly/ApplicantRegistrationWebsite

• Step 2: Pay the registration fee of P300 through onsite or online payment channels: http://bit.ly/ApplicantPaymentOptionsCY2020

• Step 23 Email a clear photo of your current school ID (or any proof of enrollment) to admissions@mcm.edu.ph and send a screenshot of your payment transaction confirmation at treasury@mcm.edu.ph with the following details:

• Applicant’s Full Name

• Purpose of Payment – Registration Fee

Send an email with the following requirements to scholarship@mcm.edu.ph.

• Report Card with GWA of 90% and above and who complied with the required documents (Report Card/Certification)

• Recent ITR of both parents, with a combined annual income of P300,000 or less.

• Note: The scholarship-in-charge may verify with the Admissions Office by sending the names of those who submitted the complete scholarship requirements (Report Card with 90% GWA and ITR) to admissions@mcm.edu.ph (cc: Admissions Head).

Mode of Contact with Students: Email and Cellphone (optional)

Collection of Requirements (Medium): Email and Hard Copy

3. ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE AWARD SCHOLARSHIP

Sponsor: Malayan Colleges Mindanao (A Mapúa School), Inc.

Description: The scholarship grants are given to applicants who are granted with Highest Honors, High Honors, and Honors in a school class of at least 20 Grade 12 graduates. The basis for scholarship bracketing will be in accordance with DepEd Order no. 36, s. 2016. MCM will only accept the first two (2) students of any ranking from different schools, who shall officially enroll and comply with the required documents.

Benefits:

• With Highest Honor: 100% on tuition and L/F/D fee discount for one academic year. Average grade per quarter: 98-100

• With High Honors: 50% on tuition and L/F/D fee discount for one academic year. Average grade per quarter: 95-97

• With Honor: 25% on tuition and L/F/D fee discount for one academic year. Average grade per quarter: 90-94

Retention Policy: The student must not have failing grades and must obtain a credit term average of 2.00 or better at the end of each term for one academic year.

Requirements: The CSFA shall confirm with the MCM Registrar’s Office on the official ranking of the student.

4. EARLY BIRD DISCOUNT

Sponsor: Malayan Colleges Mindanao (A Mapúa School), Inc.

Description: The discount is granted to the first 300 MCM Grade 12 applicants to enroll or reserve a slot through the payment of the Enrollment Confirmation Fee (ECF). This scholarship is applicable on top of other MCM scholarship offerings.

Benefits: 10% discount on tuition fee only.

Retention Policy: The discount is good for one term only.

5. YGC & AYALA PROMOTIONAL DISCOUNT

Sponsor: Malayan Colleges Mindanao (A Mapúa School), Inc.

Description: Either parent must be a regular employee with good standing of any YGC or Ayala company. Applications must be submitted to the Centers for Scholarship and Financial Assistance of MCM every term and must meet the deadline set by the HR and CSFA.

Benefits: 25% discount on tuition and L/F/D fees.

Requirements:

• Certificate of Employment of parent (every semester)

• YGC or Ayala form and waiver (provided by MCM) signed by the HR of the company (every semester)

• PSA Marriage Certificate (photocopy)

• PSA Birth Certificate (photocopy)

6. SIBLING DISCOUNT

Sponsor: Malayan Colleges Mindanao (A Mapúa School), Inc.

Description: The sibling discount may be availed by a college student with at least one sibling currently enrolled at MCM. The students should be full siblings (not step or half-siblings). Note: In case the older sibling drops out and/or transfers, the discount will be pro-rated accordingly.

Benefits:

• Younger of two – 10% discount on tuition fee

• Youngest of three – 25% discount on tuition fee

• Youngest of four – 40% discount on tuition fee

Requirements:

• PSA Birth certificate of student and sibling/s currently enrolled in MCM (photocopy)

7. DEAN’S LIST

Sponsor: Malayan Colleges Mindanao (A Mapúa School), Inc.

Description:At the end of each regular term, all colleges of MCM will recognize the exemplary academic achievement of all their students through the Dean’s List.

Qualifications: The qualifications to become a Dean’s Lister are as follows.

• Must have a Term-Weighted Average (TWA) of 1.75 or better.

• Must not have any grade of “5.00”, “FA”, “F”, “INC”, or “C.”

• Must be included in the Top 5% of the total program population. The Top 5% of the total program population with a 0.8 decimal percentage or higher will be rounded off to the next whole number.

• Must not have received any disciplinary actions or sanctions.

Note: The Deans must endorse the list of programs with less than the required number of units load per term or semester to the CSFA for them to be considered.

Category and Benefits:

• Partial Academic Scholarship: Dean’s Lister with TWA of 1.75 to 1.51 and belonging to the Top 5% of his/her program’s total population will be entitled to a 50% discount on tuition and laboratory fees for the succeeding regular term.

• Full Academic Scholarship: Dean’s Lister with TWA of 1.50 to 1.00 and belonging to the Top 5% of his/her program’s total population will be entitled to a full discount on tuition and laboratory fees for the succeeding regular term.

8. JOSE RIZAL SCHOLARSHIP

Sponsor: Center for Service-Learning and Community Engagement (CSCE)

Description: The Jose Rizal Scholarship is awarded to any applicant who has a passion for community service and adheres to the core advocacies of the institution. The program aims to impart the spirit of nationalism, volunteerism, environmental, and socio-economic awareness amongst its grantees. Applications must be submitted to the Center for Service-Learning and Community Engagement office every term and must meet the deadline set by the CSCE and Scholarship offices.

Benefits: 50% Discount on tuition and laboratory fees and may avail of the scholarship for a maximum of two (2) terms.

Retention Policy: First NSTP term should have at least a TWA of 2.5.

Qualifications:

• Must be a Filipino citizen.

• Must be enrolled in an NSTP course.

• The annual family income (combined income of parents and/or guardians) must not exceed P500,000.

9. STUDY AID

Sponsor: Malayan Colleges Mindanao (A Mapúa School), Inc.

Description: The grant is applicable to up to 3 legitimate children of regular employees (teaching and non-teaching). It is applicable up to the total number of units prescribed by the program. In case the student transfers from one program to another, the grant is applicable to the total number of units of the program with a lesser number of units.

Benefits: 50% Discount on tuition fees on all MMC programs for up to 3 legitimate children. The remaining 50% plus other fees relevant to the enrollment of the child may be paid thru salary deduction but must be fully paid within the term

Retention Policy: Regular employment of the employee.
